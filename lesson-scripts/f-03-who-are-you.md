# Lesson f-03 — "Who Are You? Introduce Yourself Completely in French"

**Target length:** 11–13 minutes (~1,850 words) — full-coverage teaching version
**HeyGen avatar:** Sophie (warm, professional French-Canadian teacher)
**Speaking speed:** 0.95x
**Tone:** Warm, thorough, encouraging — a real French teacher who explains EVERY word, gives you time to write it down, and cheers you on

> **Teaching principles (every lesson):**
> 1. Teach EVERYTHING in the lesson — every vocab item, not just highlights.
> 2. For each: say it slowly → meaning → real Canadian example → learner repeats.
> 3. Give NOTE-TAKING time — tell them to write it down, then pause `[PAUSE 4 sec]`.
> 4. Encourage constantly — normalise mistakes, name their progress, keep it warm.
