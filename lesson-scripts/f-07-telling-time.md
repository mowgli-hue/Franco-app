**(TEXT: et quart = :15)**

**(TEXT: moins le quart = :45)**

And the clever one — quarter [EMPHASIS]to[/EMPHASIS] — [SLOW][EMPHASIS]moins le quart.[/EMPHASIS][/SLOW] [PAUSE 1 sec] "Moins" means "minus." **(TEXT: 24-hour clock: 14h = 2 PM, 18h = 6 PM)**

And now the 24-hour clock — the one on your appointment card. [PAUSE 1 sec] Official Quebec uses it. After noon, just keep counting: 1 p.m. is thirteen, 2 p.m. is fourteen. [PAUSE 1 sec] So [SLOW][EMPHASIS]quatorze heures trente[/EMPHASIS][/SLOW] — fourteen-thirty — is [EMPHASIS]two-thirty in the afternoon.[/EMPHASIS] The trick: after twelve, subtract twelve.
